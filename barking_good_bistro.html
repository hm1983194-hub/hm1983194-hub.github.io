Barking Good Bistro

European Bistro Style
4.8 (320 reviews)

Barking Good Bistro is an elegant European-style bistro located in the heart of Central. Our restaurant offers a cozy yet sophisticated atmosphere where both you and your four-legged companion can enjoy a memorable dining experience. With a menu featuring classic European cuisine made with premium ingredients and a dedicated pet-friendly terrace, we aim to provide the perfect setting for quality time with your pet while enjoying exquisite food and drinks.
