Top 10 Pet-Friendly Restaurants in New York City

By Sarah Johnson Published on October 15, 2023 8 min read
A dog enjoying a meal at a pet-friendly restaurant

Finding restaurants that welcome both you and your furry companion can be challenging in a bustling city like New York. But worry not! We've curated a list of the top 10 pet-friendly restaurants where you and your pet can enjoy a wonderful dining experience together.

From cozy cafes with special pet menus to upscale restaurants with outdoor seating designed for pets, New York offers a variety of options for pet owners. These establishments not only allow pets but go the extra mile to make them feel welcome with water bowls, treats, and sometimes even special pet menus.

When dining with your pet, it's important to follow proper etiquette to ensure a pleasant experience for everyone. Always call ahead to confirm the restaurant's pet policy, keep your pet on a leash, and be mindful of other diners.

Our top picks include The Barking Lot Bistro in Greenwich Village, known for its homemade dog treats and spacious outdoor patio, and Paws & Plates in Brooklyn, which offers a separate menu for dogs with healthy, vet-approved options.

Many of these restaurants also host pet-friendly events throughout the year, such as "Yappy Hours" and costume contests during holidays. These events are a great way to meet other pet owners and socialize your furry friend in a safe, welcoming environment.

When visiting any pet-friendly restaurant, remember to bring essential items like a leash, waste bags, and a portable water bowl (though most establishments will provide these). It's also a good idea to check if the restaurant requires reservations, especially during peak hours or for larger groups.
