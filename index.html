PAW GARDEN

Recommended Restaurant
4.8 (245 reviews)

PAW GARDEN is a garden restaurant designed specifically for pets and their owners, offering spacious outdoor areas and comfortable indoor environments. Our menu features not only delicious human food but also specially designed healthy meals for pets. All areas are equipped with pet-friendly facilities, including water stations, rest areas, and play spaces.

Our chef team carefully prepares each dish using fresh organic ingredients to ensure both you and your pet enjoy a high-quality dining experience. We also have live music performances on weekends to add more fun to your meal.
